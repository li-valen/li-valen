## Available Colors
You can change the color parameter to any of these:
- `blue` (default)
- `red`
- `green`
- `yellow`
- `orange`
- `purple`
- `pink`
- `black`
- `white`

## Available Styles
- `for-the-badge` - Large, bold style
- `flat-square` - Clean, modern style
- `plastic` - 3D effect style
- `flat` - Simple flat style
